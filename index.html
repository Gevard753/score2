About SCORE Token

SCORE is a utility token launched on Solana and designed for use in games, lotteries, reputation systems, and reward distributions. In the future, it will become the foundation of a new blockchain network built for scalability, transparency, and decentralization.

  • Deployed on Solana
  • Used in mini-apps, games, and P2E systems
  • Phantom and mobile wallet support
  • Planned migration to our own network
